SIKIZANA CHAMA BYLAWS (SAMPLE)

1. MEMBERSHIP
- Every member must pay a one-time registration fee of 1,000 KES.
- Monthly contributions are set at 2,000 KES, payable by the 5th of every month.

2. PENALTIES
- Late monthly contributions attract a fine of 200 KES per week of delay.
- Failure to attend a monthly meeting without a valid reason attracts a fine of 500 KES.

3. LOANS
- Members are eligible for loans after 6 months of active contribution.
- Maximum loan amount is 3 times the member's total savings.
- Interest rate is 10% per month on a reducing balance basis.
- Repayment period is a maximum of 12 months.

4. WITHDRAWALS
- A member can withdraw from the chama by giving a 2-month written notice.
- Withdrawing members receive their total contributions minus any outstanding debts and a 5% administrative fee.
- Refunds are processed within 90 days of the notice period expiry.

5. DISPUTE RESOLUTION
- All disputes shall first be mediated by the chama executive committee.
- If mediation fails, an independent arbitrator or an AI mediation agent (Sikizana) may be consulted.
- The decision of the arbitrator shall be binding if agreed upon by a 2/3 majority of the members.
